## Hard-won fact that changes plan (details in docs/issues/005)

**cargo-deny `[[bans.deny]]` + `wrappers` is graph-wide**: EVERY direct
parent of a banned crate (including external crates like uuid, tempfile,
proptest, rustls, sqlx-*) must be a wrapper or the check fails. It cannot
express "only these four service crates may not directly depend on X."
Verified empirically with 0.20.2: the issue-002 config errors 7 times on
unmodified opus/m1-proto, and worse once sqlx merges (sqlx-mysql/postgres
directly pull hkdf, hmac, md-5, rand, sha1, sha2, rsa). The replacement is
the scoped `cargo metadata --no-deps` check on the spike branch — and it is
**not blocked on the facade merging** (it inspects service manifests).
